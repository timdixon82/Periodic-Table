# Project Wiki: Glossary: Periodic-Table

Domain terms specific to Periodic-Table, defined as the team meets them. Terms that apply across every project are in the global wiki's glossary.

## Element

A chemical element: a substance made of one kind of atom, identified by its atomic number. The periodic table arranges all known elements.

## Atomic number

The number of protons in an atom's nucleus. It uniquely identifies a chemical element and sets its position in the periodic table.

## Group

A vertical column of the periodic table. Elements in the same group tend to share chemical properties.

## Period

A horizontal row of the periodic table.
